Why Choose Tomato?

1 Tomatoes are one of the most widely grown and consumed crops worldwide. Early detection of leaf diseases can prevent widespread crop damage, ensuring healthy growth and maintaining high yields.

2Traditional disease detection methods involve manual inspection by experts, which is time-consuming, labor-intensive, and prone to human error. An automated model can provide consistent and rapid results.

3 By identifying diseases early, farmers can apply targeted treatments, reducing the unnecessary use of chemical pesticides. This helps promote environmentally friendly farming and reduces production costs
